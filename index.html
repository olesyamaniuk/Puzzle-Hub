FAQ

  • How do I start playing?

    Download Puzzle Hub from Google Play and start with the daily challenge or select a level from the main menu

    Yes, optional purchases are available for hints, power-ups, and premium puzzles

  • Can I play offline?

    Yes, most puzzles are available offline, but an internet connection is required for daily challenges and updates

  • What types of puzzles are included?

    Puzzle Hub features jigsaws, logic puzzles, word games, number puzzles, and more

  • Is there a multiplayer mode?

    No, Puzzle Hub is designed for solo play, focusing on personal brain training and relaxation
